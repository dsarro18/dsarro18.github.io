Advancement

October 26, 2025

Does anyone still do their company’s accounting by hand?

What about watching a transistor television?

Or owning a cell phone without internet access?

Outside of a few very, very aged individuals, the answer is NO, and why would you?

As society “progresses” through the 21st century, only those wishing to be left behind fail to embrace the latest technology. Advancement. Efficiency. Innovation.

Yet the paradox is how destructive this progress truly is.

The software that automates your accounting erases the very calculation and analysis skills that once made you competent.

Your new ultra-HD television delivers crystal-clear SLOP, an endless feed of hollow films and shows.

Your smartphone opens every corner of the digital world while closing off genuine human connection.

Those who refuse to embrace this self-destruction perish faster than those who accept it.

Would you like to plunge to your death in fifty feet, or one hundred?

The choice, as always, is yours.
