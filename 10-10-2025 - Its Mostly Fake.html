It’s (Mostly) Fake

October 10, 2025

Jobs, history, entertainment, social relationships…

What percentage of any of it is real?

Fake jobs prop up a fake economy
to earn fake titles
to flex fake social status.

And the worst part?
It’s all mandatory.
Try stepping outside of it…
good luck surviving.

Once you see it for what it is,
you can’t unsee it.
